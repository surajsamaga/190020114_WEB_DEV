Activities

Mars Rover Team

I am a member of the Electrical and Software Subsystem in the Mars Rover Team. The objective of the team is to design a Rover capable of investigating and navigating terrains like that of Mars. Head over to Mars Rover Team IIT Bombay for insight on the cool things we do!

Reading Text from the Wild

I undertook this project as part of the Seasons of Code initiative by the Web and Coding Club, IIT Bombay. The objective was to develop a CNN based OCR to detect and recognize text from images taken in the wild. I worked on the Google SVT Dataset, and used the YOLOv2 algorithm for text detection, and a custom designed CNN for text recognition. Find the implementation details in this github repo.
