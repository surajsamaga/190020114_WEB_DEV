Activities

Mars Rover Team IIT Bombay

As a member of the Mars Rover Team @IIT-B, I am tasked with designing, exploring & experimenting with the Electrical and Software Subsystem. We strive to build a prototype mars rover capable of investigating and navigating Mars like terrains that could potentially help astronauts to explore the ‘red planet’. We shall be presenting our rover in the University Rover Challenge, Utah, USA. While the challenges are galore, head over to Mars Rover Team IIT Bombay for more insights into our work!

Reading Text from the Wild

I undertook this project as part of the Seasons of Code initiative by the Web and Coding Club, IIT Bombay. The objective was to develop a Convolutional Neural Network based OCR to detect and recognize text from images taken in the wild. Sounds interesting? Head over to Github for the implementational details.
